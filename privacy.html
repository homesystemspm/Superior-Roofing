Privacy policy

This website collects only the information a visitor chooses to provide through the inspection request form or direct phone contact. The goal is to respond to project inquiries and operate the site safely.

Information collected

  • Name, phone number, city, selected project type, and optional project notes submitted through the request form
  • Basic technical logs produced by the hosting platform, such as IP address, browser type, and page requests

How information is used

  • To respond to roofing inquiries and schedule follow-up conversations
  • To protect the site from spam, abuse, and broken submissions
  • To maintain and improve the site experience

Sharing

Information is not sold. It may be shared only with service providers needed to host the website or deliver a form submission endpoint once that configuration is enabled.

Retention

Inquiry information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to respond to the request, maintain business records, or satisfy legal obligations.
